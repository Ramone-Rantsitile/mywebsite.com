Due to my backgound, cars have always been the things i loved the most. My father was a car guy since I was born and he loved cars so much even to date. He used to buy me many car toys when I was still a child and thats when and how my love for cars began. Time past by and I became older and older and therefore let away the toys and started making wire-cars, due to my fantastic creativity I would make some awesome wire-cars that non of my agemates in my village would build especialy at that age.

My father would award me every time I came up with new interesting features on my wire-cars, like carefully placed and functioning head and tail lights, spoilers etc. He would call me to watch and learn when he was fixing his car at home, sometimes I would find and fix some minor problems myself From about 11 to 13 years old, every morning when my mother was about to go to work I would start the car for her while she got ready. I became much older and went to driving school, made a driver's licence and that was the happiest time of my life, now that I can drive freely within SADC regions.
