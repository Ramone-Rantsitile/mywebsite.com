MY BACKGROUND


My name is Rants'itile Ramone. I am a 20 year old man from Thaba-Tseka.

I started my primary school at Mohlakeng Primary School from standard one. After completing standard five I was transfered

to Katlehong Primary School where I made my standard six and standard seven which I passed very well with First class. I proceeded to high school at Masowe High School in Maseru in 2015.

In 2017 I completed my Form C level and I passed well with first class. After two years, 2019 I completed my Form E and I passed very well with credit in all the seven subjects.

In 2020 I applied and was admitted at Center For Accounting Studies (CAS) under the ACCA programme but withdrew early 2021. Later I applied and was admitted at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology persuing BSc in Software Engineering with Multimedia programme
